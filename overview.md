# Mentora - Offline AI Assistant for Students

![Mentora Logo](https://via.placeholder.com/150) <!-- Replace with actual logo if available -->

**Mentora** is a premium, privacy-focused educational platform designed to empower students to learn any way they choose. By leveraging local AI, Mentora provides deep insights and interactive comprehension tools for all study materials—entirely offline.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Unified Learning Dashboard (Home)
- **Continue Learning:** Instantly resume where you left off with smart progress tracking.
- **Quick Upload:** Effortlessly import materials from various sources (PDF, DOCX, Audio, Image).
- **Recent Activity:** Keep track of your latest documents and study sessions.

### 2. Intelligent Library
- **Format Agnostic:** Support for PDF, DOCX, Audio (MP3, M4A, WAV), and Images (JPG, PNG).
- **Organization:** Categorize and filter materials by type and progress.
- **Progress Visibility:** See exactly how much of each book or document you've mastered.

### 3. AI-Powered Reader & Comprehension
- **Interactive Reading:** A sleek reader interface optimized for focus.
- **Comprehension Chat:** Ask questions directly to your study material. Get explanations, examples, and contextual summaries.
- **Quiz Mode:** Test your knowledge with AI-generated quizzes based on the specific content you're reading.
- **Annotation Tools:** Highlight key concepts and take persistent notes.

### 4. Progress Analytics & Stats
- **Overall Progress:** Visual tracking of total hours learned and books completed.
- **Detailed Insights:** Analyze study patterns by day, month, or year.
- **Subject Breakdown:** Understand which subjects you spend the most time on.
- **Activity Heatmaps:** Track your daily engagement levels.

### 5. Gamified Achievements
- **Milestones:** Earn badges for consistency, deep work, and high engagement (e.g., "Knowledge Seeker", "Night Owl").
- **Motivation:** Visual progress on achievement unlocks to keep learning momentum high.

---

## 🛠 Technical Highlights

- **Local AI Inference:** All AI processing happens on-device. No data ever leaves the user's phone, ensuring total privacy and offline availability.
- **Multi-Modal Support:**
  - **PDF/DOCX:** Intelligent text parsing.
  - **Audio:** On-device speech-to-text for lectures and audiobooks.
  - **Images:** Advanced OCR for scanning physical notes or textbooks.
- **Cross-Platform:** Built with Flutter for a consistent, high-performance experience across iOS and Android.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Mentora features a **Premium Dark Aesthetic** with:
- **Glassmorphism:** Elegant transparency and blur effects for a modern feel.
- **Vibrant Accents:** Consistent use of deep purples and high-contrast highlights.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** A streamlined bottom navigation bar for quick access to Home, Library, Progress, and Settings.
- **Dynamic Interactions:** Subtle micro-animations and smooth transitions for a buttery-smooth UX.
